Proposed Amendment:

Article XIII: Treaties and Agreements
   1. Gremlantis may enter into treaties, alliances, or formal agreements with other entities.
   2. A treaty or agreement may be initiated by:
   * The Head of State
   * The High Council
   3. All treaties and agreements must be documented in writing and shall include:
   * The parties involved
   * The terms and obligations of each party
   * The duration or conditions for termination
   4. A treaty or agreement shall require a three-fifths vote of all active members of the High Council to be ratified.
   5. The Head of State shall oversee the enforcement and interpretation of all ratified treaties but shall not participate in voting on their approval.
   6. Any treaty that conflicts with this Constitution shall be null and void.
   7. The High Council may revoke or modify a treaty through the same voting process required for its ratification.
   8. For a treaty to become valid, it is required that the representatives for all parties involved sign the final document.
   9. All treaties shall be permanently archived and made accessible to the Republic.
