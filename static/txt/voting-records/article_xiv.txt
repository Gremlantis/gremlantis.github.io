Proposed Amendment:

Article XIV: Declaration of War
   1. A declaration of war may be initiated by:
   * The Head of State
   * The High Council
   2. Any declaration of war must be presented in the form of a formal written document that:
   * Is clearly worded
   * Is complete
   * Identifies the entity against which war is declared
   * States the reasons and objectives of the declaration
   3. A declaration of war shall require a three-fifths vote of all active members of the High Council to take effect.
   4. The Head of State may initiate the process and oversee its execution, but shall not participate in voting on the declaration.
   5. Emergency actions taken under Article VIII shall not constitute a formal declaration of war unless approved under this Article.
   6. All declarations of war shall be permanently archived and made accessible to the Republic.
