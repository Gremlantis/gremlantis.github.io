Treaty of Peace
The Sovereign State of Gremlantis


Recognizing the shared history of the recent conflict known as The Great Banana War, and acknowledging that the tensions arising from the mass production and distribution of potassium-laden bananas were rooted in satire and communal engagement.
The Sovereign State of Gremlantis and the Banana Republic hereby enter into this Peace Treaty in the interest of stability, humor, and unity across the lands of Discordia.
Article I: Resolution of Hostilities
1. All ceremonial hostilities, wartime proclamations, and declarations between the Sovereign State of Gremlantis and the Banana Republic shall formally cease upon the signing of this Treaty.
2. The Great Banana War is hereby recognized by both parties as having concluded in an honorable draw.
3. Neither nation shall claim exclusive victory nor impose terms of surrender upon the other.
4. No further actions shall be taken under the banner of war following ratification, and the conflict shall be preserved solely as historical record and community lore.
Article II: Potassium and Diplomatic Relations
1. All grievances concerning the production, hoarding, and distribution of potassium-laden bananas are hereby declared resolved.
2. No future declarations of war shall be made on the basis of potassium without the mutual consent of both parties.
3. The Sovereign State of Gremlantis and the Banana Republic agree to restore peaceful and respectful relations.
4. All communication shall proceed in good faith and without escalation.
5. Any future disputes shall be addressed through diplomacy, humor, and dialogue rather than conflict.
Article III: Preservation of History and Ratification
1. This Treaty shall stand as the official conclusion of the Great Banana War and may be commemorated in lore, archives, and the collective memory of Discordia.
2. It shall serve as a lasting symbol of unity following a period of ceremonial rivalry.
3. This Treaty shall take effect immediately upon signature by representatives of both parties.
4. The date of signing shall thereafter be recognized as the official end to the Great Banana War.
Signed on this day for the purpose of concluding hostilities and restoring peace among Discordia.
